About Missing Piece

Our comprehensive, turn-key solution allows you to devote more time to your patients and to growing your practice.  Let us help you increase revenue, shorten collection time, and eliminate the administrative burden of in-house billing operations.

The Missing Piece Solution

Implementation

Implementing a new billing system can be overwhelming. Missing Piece leads you through this process for a smooth transition.

Credentialing & Contracting

Our Contracting Team’s knowledge and payer relationships establish a strong foundation for your revenue cycle so you receive maximum reimbursement.

Eligibility & Benefits

Navigating patient benefits is challenging and time consuming. We manage this process so your patients understand their benefits and your billing is accurate.

Pre-Authorization

Most payers require pre-authorization for ABA services. Missing Piece works with payers to obtain authorization for full recommended treatment for your patients.

Claims Management

We proactively manage claims submitted, reducing the time between claim submission and payment.

Remittance Posting

Accounts Receivable Follow-up

Appeals Management

Our appeal experience and ABA medical necessity knowledge enables us to successfully overturn denials and collect payments that rightfully belong to you.

Patient Billing
