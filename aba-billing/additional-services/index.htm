Speech Therapy and Occupational Therapy

Our Revenue Cycle Management Solution also serves as an important component for Speech Therapy and Occupational Therapy. With Missing Piece, you won’t have to worry about insurance reimbursement and your patients can get the services they need.

Behavioral Health Services

According to a 2014 study published in The Journal of the American Medical Association, about 55 percent of psychiatrists take insurance compared to 89 percent of all other healthcare providers. One reason is the stress and frustration of obtaining reimbursement from insurance. Missing Piece is uniquely positioned to help you explore the pros and cons of accepting private insurance, and we have the knowledge to bill for mental and behavioral health professionals including LMHC, LMFT, LCSW, HSPP, PsyD, PhD. Using the same best practices and technology, we manage the complexities of the revenue cycle so you can focus on your patients.

Telehealth

With the ever-changing face of healthcare, our expertise extends beyond traditional methods of therapy delivery to include innovative approaches such as telehealth services. We have the necessary knowledge and experience with telehealth benefits and billing to ensure you can offer your services to patients remotely and receive maximum reimbursement.

If you currently offer telehealth services, we can assist with your eligibility, authorization, billing, and other needs. If you do not currently offer this service but want to learn more, we are here to help you understand the requirements and reimbursement opportunities so that you are prepared and informed.
