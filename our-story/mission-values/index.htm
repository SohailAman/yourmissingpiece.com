Mission & Values

Our Mission

Missing Piece is committed to delivering turn-key billing and consulting solutions that enable clinicians to devote more time to delivering the best care and outcomes to individuals with autism.

Our Values

Collaboration and Teamwork

We work as a team, using our collective talents to exceed the expectations of our provider customers.

Transparency & Clear Communication

We believe honesty and openness creates trust with our customers and between our team members.

Accountability

We foster a culture of ownership of the processes entrusted to us and pursue maximum reimbursement for our provider customers.

Commitment to Family & Children

Our goal is to remove the administrative burden of billing from our provider customers so they can focus on improving the lives of children and their families. We also recognize the importance of family to our employees by promoting a family-friendly workplace.
